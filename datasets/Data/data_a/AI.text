Artificial Intelligence (AI) stands for Artificial Intelligence.

AI is a field of computer science that focuses on building systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

These tasks include:
- Learning from data
- Understanding language
- Recognizing images
- Making decisions
- Solving problems

There are different types of AI:

1) Narrow AI (Weak AI):
   Designed for specific tasks.
   Examples: Chatbots, recommendation systems, voice assistants.

2) General AI (Strong AI):
   A theoretical type of AI that can perform any intellectual task a human can do.
   It does not yet exist.

3) Superintelligent AI:
   A hypothetical AI that surpasses human intelligence.

Common AI techniques include:

- Machine Learning (ML): Systems learn patterns from data.
- Deep Learning: A subset of ML using neural networks.
- Natural Language Processing (NLP): Understanding and generating human language.
- Computer Vision: Understanding images and videos.
- Reinforcement Learning: Learning through trial and error.

AI applications include:
- Healthcare (disease prediction)
- Finance (fraud detection)
- Autonomous vehicles
- Virtual assistants
- Recommendation systems

AI systems improve by:
- Training on large datasets
- Using better embeddings
- Optimizing models
- Improving retrieval systems (like RAG)

AI is widely used in modern software systems and powers many intelligent applications today.
